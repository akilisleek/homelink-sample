Admin dashboard concept

Internal operations workspace

This dashboard demonstrates how HomelinkGlobal could manage listings, inquiries, bookings, users, and relocation-service requests from one secure administrative area.

Operations flow

1Agent or owner submits a property listing for review.
2Internal team verifies details, media, pricing, ownership status, and location.
3Verified property becomes visible in the marketplace and can receive inquiries.
4Customer inquiries are assigned to an agent, viewing is scheduled, and follow-up status is tracked.

Production modules

Recommended full version modules include authentication, roles and permissions, property CRUD, media uploads, inquiry CRM, booking calendar, service providers, payments, country configuration, analytics, and audit logs.
